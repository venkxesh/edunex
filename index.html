A Message from the Founder

Hello,

I’m Venkatesh Tolambiya, a 15-year-old student entrepreneur, AI innovator, and founder of EduNex, currently studying in Class 10. I am widely recognized as the AI Kid of India and Rajasthan’s Youngest AI Prodigy.

Most people believe that innovation begins after college or years of professional experience. But I believe that impact can start early.

My journey began when I started exploring technology and coding at the age of 10. What began as curiosity soon turned into a mission — to rethink how students learn and prepare for the future.

Today’s education system still focuses heavily on memorization, marks, and examinations, while the real world is rapidly moving toward artificial intelligence, technology, creativity, and practical skills. I founded EduNex to help bridge this gap.

EduNex is not just a platform — it is a vision for the future of education.

Our mission is to help students move beyond textbooks and become future-ready thinkers, creators, and innovators. Through EduNex, we aim to introduce students to AI, technology, real-world learning, and practical problem-solving skills that truly prepare them for tomorrow.

While continuing my regular school journey as a 10th-grade student, I remain committed to building tools, platforms, and initiatives that empower young minds to learn, build, and innovate from an early age.

I strongly believe that age is not a barrier when the vision is strong and the purpose is clear.

EduNex represents a generation of students who are ready to think differently, create boldly, and shape the future.

This is only the beginning.

— Venkatesh Tolambiya
